Understanding Phishing Attacks

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers deceive victims into revealing sensitive information by posing as legitimate entities. These attacks have become increasingly sophisticated, targeting individuals, businesses, and organizations worldwide.

Types of Phishing Attacks

Email Phishing

  • • Fake login pages
  • • Account verification scams
  • • Payment fraud attempts
  • • Credential harvesting

Spear Phishing

  • • Targeted attacks
  • • Company-specific scams
  • • Executive targeting
  • • Social engineering

Smishing & Vishing

  • • SMS-based attacks
  • • Voice phishing calls
  • • Mobile malware
  • • Text message scams
